# Ark of Osiris Strategy Planner

Plan and coordinate your alliance's 30v30 Ark of Osiris battles with interactive maps, team assignments, and phase-based strategy guides.

## Overview

Ark of Osiris is Rise of Kingdoms' premier 30v30 alliance battle mode. Teams compete to control structures, capture altars, and hold the Ark to score points. The Strategy Planner helps you:

- Organize 30 players into specialized zone-based teams
- Assign buildings with attack phase ordering
- Visualize positions on an interactive battle map
- Generate shareable strategy guides for Discord/chat

## Features

### Interactive Battle Map

The map displays all 18 strategic buildings:

| Building Type | Count | Purpose |
|--------------|-------|---------|
| **Obelisks** | 4 | High-value capture points |
| **Outposts of Iset** | 3 | Your team's starting structures |
| **Outposts of Seth** | 3 | Enemy team's structures |
| **Shrines of War** | 2 | Combat buff shrines |
| **Shrines of Life** | 2 | Healing shrines |
| **Desert Altars** | 2 | Mid-map capture points |
| **Sky Altars** | 2 | Elevated capture points |
| **Ark** | 1 | Central objective for points |

### Zone-Based Team System

Players are organized into three colored zones:

| Zone | Color | Position | Typical Focus |
|------|-------|----------|---------------|
| **Zone 1** | Blue | Lower/Left | Obelisk (Left), Iset structures |
| **Zone 2** | Orange | Center | Ark control, center altars |
| **Zone 3** | Purple | Upper/Right | Obelisk (Upper), Seth push |

### Four-Phase Battle System

Each battle follows four distinct phases with specific objectives:

1. **Phase 1: RUSH** (0:00) - Fastest marches grab obelisks
2. **Phase 2: SECURE** (~5:00) - Teleport waves, establish control
3. **Phase 3: ARK + FORTIFY** (~15:00) - Capture Ark, fill garrisons
4. **Phase 4: HOLD & PUSH** (~45:00) - Defend and selective offense

## Quick Start

1. Navigate to **AoO Strategy** from the homepage
2. Use **Find My Role** to look up your assignment
3. Review the **Quick Guide** for phase instructions
4. Check the **Strategy Map** for building positions
5. **Copy Strategy** to share with your alliance
